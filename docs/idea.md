# Project Idea

Food Waste Tracker is a mobile-first web app for helping people keep track of food before it expires.

Many people forget what they already have at home, buy duplicate items, or discover food only after it has gone bad. This app is planned as a simple tool for tracking food items, expiry dates, shopping needs, and food waste over time.

## Main Problem

Food waste often happens because people lose track of:

- what food they have
- when food expires
- what needs to be used soon
- what they already need to buy
- which items are commonly wasted

## Target User

The app is for people who want a simple way to manage food at home, especially on a phone while shopping, cooking, or checking the fridge.

## Core Idea

Users will be able to add food items, give them expiry dates, and see which items need attention soon. They will also be able to mark food as consumed or wasted so the app can later show useful waste statistics.

## Later Ideas

Later versions may include food photos, improved statistics, PWA support, and other features once the core app is working.
